# 📌 Project Overview

The **Rapido Ride Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to transform raw ride-booking data into meaningful business insights.

This dashboard enables businesses to monitor operational performance, analyze customer travel behavior, track revenue trends, and identify high-demand locations through intuitive visualizations.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

✔ Monitor overall ride performance

✔ Analyze monthly revenue trends

✔ Track completed and cancelled rides

✔ Discover popular pickup & destination locations

✔ Identify peak booking hours

✔ Analyze service-wise ride distribution

✔ Explore top revenue-generating routes

# 🔍 Key Insights

📌 Highest ride volume observed in **July**

📌 Revenue peaked during **July**

📌 Bike rides are the most preferred service

📌 Banaswadi recorded the highest pickup demand

📌 UPI is the most frequently used payment method

📌 Evening hours (6 PM–12 AM) receive maximum bookings

📌 Frequently travelled routes generate the highest revenue
